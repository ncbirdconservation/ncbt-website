Wildlife-Focused Education Resources

This brochure is a resource for formal and informal educators who want to teach their students more about wildlife and habitat. Enclosed is information about curriculum and classroom activities; teacher workshop and training opportunities; program and field trip opportunities; grants and fellowships; and, images and materials.
