North Carolina Birding Trail

The North Carolina Birding Trail links 300+ bird watching sites and birders with communities, businesses and educational attractions. But it is more than lines on a map, and birders do more than seek out rare birds. They help others learn about birds. They help scientists collect data. They can have a benefit local economies. The North Carolina Birding Trail is a network in many ways, for the benefit of all.
