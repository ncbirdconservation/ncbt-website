Description

Turnbull Creek Educational State Forest encompassing 890 acres offers a variety of habitats from open fields to oak forest and mixed hardwood/pines where songbirds might be spotted. The forest also includes a picnic shelter (available by reservation) and a Fire Control Exhibit. Like other educational forests, Turnbull Creek has been developed for use as an living outdoor class room. Its purpose is to teach children and adults about the complex, interdependent ecosystems which make up a forest and which can be managed for a multitude of uses. Turnbull Creek Educational State Forest includes demonstration of forestry practices designed to restore historic conditions, like the Longleaf Pine Shelterwood Cut area. View the trail map for more information.

Habitat

flat coastal plain forest with small bays, dry coniferous woodlands, oak forest & mixed hardwood/pine, early successional, dry longleaf pine, pocosin, small wetland communities and nonalluvial wetlands.

Special Features/Concerns

Hunting is allowed in the Turnbull Creek Educational State Forest during certain times of the year. The forest will be closed during youth and adult hunts. Birders should be aware of current hunting regulations and seasons, and take adequate safety precautions during those times. For more on hunting precautions, see the hunting season information at the beginning of this guide. Snakes and biting insect are prevalent during warm weather. Pets are permitted but must be leashed. Horses and ATVs are not allowed.

Access and Parking

The long trail is about 4 miles. The short trail along Turnbull Creek is about 500-600 yards long. The terrain consists of dirt/grass and both open woods and deep woods. The State Forest is open Mid-March until Mid-November. Hours are: 8am to 6pm Monday-Friday; Saturday by appointment; Sunday closed. Parking is available at multiple locations along the entrance road.

Directions

From Elizabethtown, take Hwy 701N. Turn left onto Highway 53. Make an immediate right onto Highway 242. Turn right onto Sweet Home Church Road. Entrance and office will be approximately 100 yards on the right.
