Description

Mitchell's Nursery and Greenhouse is a local, family-owned shrub and tree nursery with greenhouses on the way to Pilot Mountain from Winston-Salem. The best birding areas on the small 2-acre site are found in the potted trees and along the periphery of the property, where you'll find typical birds such as Eastern Bluebird, American Goldfinch, and Barn Swallow while perusing the variety of plants.

Species of Interest (Full Bird List)

American Goldfinches in flocks in winter and eating Crape Myrtle seeds in spring; Mourning Doves, Northern Mockingbirds, American Robins, sparrows, wrens, Eastern Bluebirds, Killdeer, Ruby-Throated Hummingbirds, and Barn Swallows.

Special Features/Concerns

Tours of the nursery and greenhouses are available. Tadpoles may be found in puddles in the spring. Pets are permitted but must be leashed.

Access and Parking

Hours: 8am-5pm Monday-Friday, 8am-4pm Saturday. Closed on Sunday. The site has 30 parking spaces.

Directions

From Exit 123 on Highway 52, travel toward Downtown King. Dalton Road goes through downtown. Turn left on Dalton Road and go approximately 2 miles. The Nursery is on the right.
